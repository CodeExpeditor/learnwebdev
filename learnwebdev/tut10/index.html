About us

Founded in 2018 by a group of environmental scientists and software engineers, Evergreen Innovations was born out of a shared frustration with the tech industry's carbon footprint. We asked ourselves: "What if cutting-edge technology could also be cutting-edge in sustainability?" This question became our guiding light.

What we do?

At Evergreen Innovations, we are dedicated to transforming the way people interact with technology by integrating sustainability into every aspect of our products and services. Our mission is to create innovative solutions that not only enhance everyday life but also contribute to a healthier planet.

Whether it's a day-long hike or a multi-week expedition, trekking offers a unique blend of physical exertion, mental resilience, and spiritual rejuvenation.
